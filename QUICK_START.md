# Quick Instructions: Press 'a' in the Metro terminal

The Metro bundler is now running successfully on port 8083.

To launch the app on your emulator, simply press the 'a' key in the Metro terminal window.

This will:
1. Build the app
2. Install it on the emulator
3. Launch it automatically

Wait 30-60 seconds for the build to complete and the app should appear on your emulator!
